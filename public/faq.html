FAQs

Will Scoregee be accessible on mobile?
At this moment, Scoregee doesn't have an app to be accessed on a mobile device. Only on computer at this time.
Will there be a sign in page?
The sign in page is a work in progress. At a later time, it'll be made, in which you can create an account, and sign into your individual account.
What's the perks of having a Scoregee account?
You'll be able to pick your favorite teams and track what days that they play.
Why does Scoregee only track soccer? Why not other sports?
We only have time to implement soccer scores at this time. Once we're done with soccer we're open to tracking other sports like basketball, football, and more.
