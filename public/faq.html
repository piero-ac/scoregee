FAQ

Will ScoreGee be accessible on mobile?
At this moment, Scoregee doesn't have an app to be accessed on a mobile device. Only on computer at this time.
Will there be a sign in page?
The sign in page is a work in progress. At a later time, it'll be made, in which you can create an account, and sign onto your individual account.
What's the perks of having a ScoreGee account?
You'll be able to track soccer scores of your favorite team(s) from the past or present.
Why does Scoregee only track soccer? Why not other sports?
We only have time to implement soccer scores at this time. Once we're done with soccer we're open to tracking other sports like basketball, football, and more.
Will ScoreGee connect to platforms in order to watch the game?
No. At this time ScoreGee only provides scores and not where or how to watch that particular game.
Is everything on ScoreGee free to use?
Yes. All of ScoreGee's perks and benefits will be given to anyone who uses it.
What if there isn't a question that isn't on this FAQ page already?
You can go to the "Contact" tab and you'll be able to see ways to contact us indivually or as a team. You can then ask us that question(s) via email or phone number.
Does ScoreGee work using Wifi or Cellular Data?
Yes. You can either use Wifi or Cellular Data to access and use ScoreGee.
What if the ScoreGee website isn't working?
Some troubleshooting solutions are: 1. Refresh the page. 2. Exit the current web browser and start it back up. 3. Restart your computer. 4. If possible, try ScoreGee on a different web browser. If anything from 1-4 doesn't work be free to contact us (view the Contact page for our contact information).
Is there a newsletter I could sign up for in order to get new ScoreGee information?
No. As of right now there isn't a newsletter you could sign up for. Once the website is much more complete we'll consider making a newsletter.
Will a search bar ever be implemented on the website?
Yes. Eventually we will add a search bar where you'll be able to search for anything soccer related into that search bar.
What makes ScoreGee different from other Sports websites?
ScoreGee is dedicated to give up to date scores for only Soccer. That is the only sport in which we're displaying information throughout on the website.
How many different soccer leagues are shown in ScoreGee?
There are 5 soccer leagues that are displayed in ScoreGee.
What soccer leagues are displayed in ScoreGee?
Premier League, Bundesliga, Ligue 1, Seria A, and La Liga.
